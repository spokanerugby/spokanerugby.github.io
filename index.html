We are the Spokane Razorbacks

Rugby has been played in Spokane since the 1970s and the Spokane Razorback Rugby Club is the current incarnation. We formed in 1996 as an alternative to the Spokane Mens Rugby Club. Our beginning was modest, but we were not. Our first tournament we won a created-just-for-us award for our enthusiasm both on and off the field. The team flourished from that point and later merged with the Spokane Mens Club.

Currently, we are a member of the Pacific Northwest Rugby Union PNRFU which holds global recognition along with over 2000 other clubs located just within the United States.
